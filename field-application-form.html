Part II: Student Accommodation Information

The University of the Pacific is committed to providing an accessible learning environment in which all course and field education experiences are usable by every student and faculty member. If you believe you require disability-related academic adjustments for your field internship (including pregnancy-related disabilities), you must contact Services for Students with Disabilities (SSD) as soon as possible to discuss eligibility. An up-to-date accommodation letter from SSD is required before any adjustments—beyond what is available to all students—can be implemented. Please note that disability-related academic adjustments are not retroactive. Students who intend to request accommodations for field internship must also notify the MSW Field Director, Dr. Halide Porras, immediately.

Part V: Employment-Related Internships

An Employment-Based Field Internship allows an MSW student to complete field education requirements at their place of employment when the agency can provide a learning experience that is distinct from the student's regular job duties. Approval of an Employment-Based Field Internship is not guaranteed.
